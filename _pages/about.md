## Biography / 个人简介

**[English]** I am currently a researcher at the Beijing Academy of Artificial Intelligence (BAAI). I received my Ph.D. from the Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CASIA), where I was advised by Prof. Shan Yu. 

My research sits at the intersection of neuroscience and artificial intelligence. I am deeply fascinated by how the biological brain achieves flexible, rapid learning and how these insights can inspire more adaptive, intelligent artificial agents. Currently, my work focuses on Brain-Computer Interfaces (BCIs), neural data analysis, and Embodied AI.

**[中文]** 目前就职于北京智源人工智能研究院（BAAI），任研究人员。在此之前，我于中国科学院自动化研究所（CASIA）获得博士学位，导师为余山研究员。

我的研究专注于神经科学与人工智能的交叉领域。我致力于探索生物脑如何实现灵活、快速的学习（Learning to learn），并利用这些神经机制启发更具自适应能力的人工智能系统。目前的主要研究方向包括：脑机接口（BCI）、神经数据分析以及具身智能（Embodied AI）。

## Research Interests / 研究兴趣

- **Brain-Computer Interfaces (BCIs) & Neural Decoding | 脑机接口与神经解码**
  Multi-dimensional neural decoding, ECoG/LFP electrophysiology analysis, and neural foundation models.  
  *(多维神经解码、ECoG/LFP 电生理信号分析、神经大模型。)*
- **Embodied AI & Primate Cognitive Mechanisms | 具身智能与灵长类认知机制**
  "Learning to learn" mechanisms, schema representation/reuse in the primate brain, and biologically-inspired AI.  
  *(“学会学习”的神经机制、灵长类大脑中的图式（Schema）表征与复用、生物启发的人工智能。)*
