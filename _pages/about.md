## Biography / 个人简介

I am currently a researcher at the Beijing Academy of Artificial Intelligence (BAAI). I received my Ph.D. from the Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CASIA), under the supervision of Prof. Shan Yu. 

Driven by a deep fascination with the intersection of neuroscience and artificial intelligence, my research focuses on bridging biological and machine intelligence. During my Ph.D., I gained extensive, full-stack experience in invasive Brain-Computer Interfaces (iBCIs) and macaque electrophysiology—spanning experimental platform construction, animal training, multi-channel data acquisition, advanced neural data analysis, and algorithmic modeling. Currently, my work focuses on Brain-Computer Interfaces (BCIs), Computational Neuroscience, and Embodied AI, dedicated to exploring how neural mechanisms can inspire adaptive intelligent agents to interact with the physical world.

目前就职于北京智源人工智能研究院（BAAI），任研究员。在此之前，我于中国科学院自动化研究所（CASIA）获得博士学位，导师为余山研究员。

我的研究专注于神经科学与人工智能的交叉領域，致力于探寻生物智能与机器智能的深度结合。博士期间，我在侵入式脑机接口与猕猴电生理研究方面积累了丰富的全栈经验，独立及合作完成了从实验平台搭建、非人灵长類动物训练、多通道电生理数据采集，到神经数据分析与算法建模的全流程工作。

立足于这一坚实的实验与分析基础，我目前的科研工作聚焦于脑机接口（BCI）、计算神经科学以及具身智能（Embodied AI），重点探索大脑如何与物理世界进行灵活高效的交互，並利用生物脑的先进机制启发新一代自适应人工智能系统。
